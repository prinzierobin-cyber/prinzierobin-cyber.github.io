The Brief

Discodaddy started as a personal idea — a brand built around the vision of creating a space where the gay community could exist at the intersection of trash and class. A place unapologetically both: chaotic and glamorous, cheap and considered.

The starting point was merch. Designing pieces that could carry the brand's attitude before the space even existed — objects that felt like artefacts of a world already in motion.

About the project

A self-initiated brand built from scratch — concept, identity and merch design all by Robin Prinzie. The visual world draws from 1970s disco culture, art deco geometry and a maximalist Miami sensibility, channelled into a system that feels both nostalgic and completely now.

The complete visual system covers logotype, typography, collateral, merch and digital presence — a brand universe designed to be worn, carried and experienced. All images were created and art directed by Robin Prinzie, produced with AI as a creative production tool.
