The Brief

Chrisma needed a brand identity that could carry both warmth and ambition — a system sophisticated enough for premium positioning, yet approachable enough to connect with a broad lifestyle audience.

The project required building a visual language from the ground up: one that could scale across packaging, campaign, retail and digital without losing coherence or character.

About the project

Full brand identity and campaign art direction for Chrisma, developed at Bureau Blanc. The project established a complete visual system — logotype, colour palette, typography and photographic language — translated across packaging design, editorial campaigns and retail touchpoints.

The identity balances refinement with personality, creating a brand presence that feels considered at every scale. Creative direction by Robin Prinzie at Bureau Blanc.
