The Brief

Solva is the world's first 12V digital wellness floor — a patented system combining UV-LED light therapy, digital grounding, and 4K immersive content within the bathroom ritual. The project required building a brand identity that could bridge cutting-edge biotech innovation with the language of premium home design.

The brief was clear: create something that felt neither clinical nor cold, but instead warm, aspirational and grounded in a genuine philosophy — Solva's core promise of "a new state of being."

About the project

Brand identity, visual system and digital design for Solva — a patented wellness technology redefining the home. The identity balances deep, space-like depth with amber gradient warmth, embodying the brand's premise: technology in service of daily human ritual.

Deliverables included the full visual system, website design, product presentation, app interface direction, and campaign visuals. The amber and sky palette, combined with Suisse Int'l typography, became the backbone of a coherent and scalable brand language. All images were created and art directed by Robin Prinzie, produced with AI as a creative production tool.
